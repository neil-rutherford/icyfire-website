Congregant referral program

Help us out!

Referring us to your church is the highest compliment we can receive. When your church subscribes to IcyFire, you get cash.

How the program works

  1. You recommend IcyFire to your church.
  2. Your church thinks we're the best thing since sliced bread and signs up.
  3. You send us an email at support@icy-fire.com and include:
    • Your name and address (so we can send you a check)
    • The name of your church (so we can verify that they signed up)
    • A selfie of you in front of the church (so we can fight fraud)
  4. We send you a check for $100.
